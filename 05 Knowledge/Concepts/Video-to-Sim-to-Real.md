# Video-to-Sim-to-Real

## Definition

Video-to-Sim-to-Real is a pipeline that converts human demonstration videos into simulation-ready task models and then into deployable robot policies.

## Why It Matters

It can reduce expensive data collection and speed up adaptation for specialized dexterous tasks.
