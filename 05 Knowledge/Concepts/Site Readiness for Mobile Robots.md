# Site Readiness for Mobile Robots

## Definition

Site readiness is the pre-deployment validation of environment geometry, floor constraints, localization conditions, and safety infrastructure required for reliable robot operation.

## Why It Matters

Most field failures are integration mismatches between robot assumptions and site reality, not algorithm defects alone.
