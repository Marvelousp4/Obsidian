# MODEX 2026 Event Sprint

## Goal

Use MODEX 2026 to collect customer signals, partner leads, product intelligence, and concrete follow-ups that directly improve customer development and robotics positioning.

## Event Facts

- Event: MODEX 2026
- Dates: 2026-04-13 to 2026-04-16
- Venue: Georgia World Congress Center, Atlanta

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Meet at least 15 high-value companies or contacts
- [ ] Capture at least 5 customer pain patterns worth turning into product or support work
- [ ] Capture at least 5 competitor / partner insights with specific implications
- [ ] Leave with clear follow-up actions for top 10 conversations

## Target Lists

### Accounts To Meet

- GreyOrange adjacent operators, integrators, and warehouse automation buyers
- Mobile robotics vendors and AMR platform teams
- Manipulation, perception, fleet, and systems integration vendors

### Questions To Validate

- What are people actually buying now vs just evaluating?
- Which AMR + manipulator use cases are landing in production?
- What are the recurring support and deployment pain points?
- What integrations, APIs, safety, and maintenance issues come up most often?

## Daily Cadence

### Before Floor Opens

- Review top booths, target people, and one backup route
- Lock 3 must-have conversations and 1 must-see seminar
- Open today's daily note and this project note

### On The Floor

- Use `Capture: Expo Conversation` for every meaningful booth discussion
- Use `Capture: Event Note` for trend or theme observations
- Use `Capture: Contact Card` immediately if a person is worth tracking

### Nightly Reset

- Link every expo conversation to an account or create a new account
- Rank follow-ups: `hot`, `strategic`, `watch`
- Turn serious opportunities into project notes
- Push Git backup before sleep
