# Wellwit Role Negotiation

## Goal

Clarify role scope, compensation, immigration support, travel policy, budget authority, operating responsibilities, and growth path before accepting or formalizing the Wellwit / Jingzhi role.

## Source

- Imported source: [[07 Resources/Work/谈话清单.txt]]

## How To Use

Use this as a prep checklist before the conversation. After the conversation, move confirmed commitments into written artifacts: offer, JD, expense policy, and project operating cadence.

## 岗位定位

### 角色定义

- Question: 这份工作到底是单点 support 还是北美项目/客户/流程搭建 owner
- Minimum expectation: 不只是纯现场support
- Target expectation: 明确包含项目推进+客户沟通+问题闭环
- Ideal expectation: 明确是北美业务0-1搭建核心人
- Preparation / notes: 先把彭总原话整理成我的理解

### Title

- Question: 我的title是什么
- Minimum expectation: Robotics Solutions Engineer
- Target expectation: Senior Robotics Solutions Engineer / North America Robotics Solutions Engineer
- Ideal expectation: 带Lead/Regional方向的title
- Preparation / notes: title要兼顾对外形象和后续发展

### 职责边界

- Question: 我具体负责哪些事
- Minimum expectation: 现有项目推进+现场问题处理
- Target expectation: 再加客户沟通+SOP+项目总表
- Ideal expectation: 再加美国端运营与团队建设基础
- Preparation / notes: 避免无限责任

### 权力

- Question: 我有哪些决策权
- Minimum expectation: 能维护项目总表并直接跟客户沟通
- Target expectation: 能协调国内资源+决定部分现场优先级
- Ideal expectation: 有美国端运营owner权限+一定预算权
- Preparation / notes: 责任必须匹配权力

## 薪酬

### Base Salary

- Question: 基础工资怎么定
- Minimum expectation: $100k base书面确认
- Target expectation: $100k并有年度review
- Ideal expectation: $100k以上+明确升级路径
- Preparation / notes: 必须W-2

### 年终奖

- Question: 有没有年终奖
- Minimum expectation: 有但先不写死比例
- Target expectation: 目标10%
- Ideal expectation: 10%-20%并有明确考核维度
- Preparation / notes: 不能只写“看表现”

### 项目奖金

- Question: 关键项目或新客户是否有额外激励
- Minimum expectation: 先有口头共识
- Target expectation: 重大项目关闭/回款有奖金
- Ideal expectation: 项目奖金+新客户奖金双机制
- Preparation / notes: 你后面会承担拓客和交付

### 调薪机制

- Question: 多久review一次
- Minimum expectation: 6-12个月review
- Target expectation: 1年内明确review和调薪标准
- Ideal expectation: 和业务增长/团队发展挂钩
- Preparation / notes: 必须写清时间点

## 雇佣形式

### 雇佣类型

- Question: 我是W-2还是1099
- Minimum expectation: W-2
- Target expectation: W-2+正规payroll
- Ideal expectation: W-2+成熟HR/payroll体系

## 保险
