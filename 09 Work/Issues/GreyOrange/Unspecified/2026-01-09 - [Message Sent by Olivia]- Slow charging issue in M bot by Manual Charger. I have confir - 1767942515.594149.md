## Problem

[Message Sent by Olivia]- Slow charging issue in M bot by Manual Charger. I have confirmed with the engineers that the root cause is the charging curve was not configured at the factory. Since the L bot and M bot use different batteries, their charging voltages are inconsistent: the L bot requires 54.6V, while the M bot requires 58.4V. Therefore, to charge the M bot normally, its charging curve must be configured, and the relevant operating SOP is as provided above. Since all chargers are set to 54.6V by default, to charge the M bot, you should select "58.4V 20A" as shown in the video tutorial and then press the Enter key. FYI
